Welcome to Rubikdom

The realm of Great Beast

Far far away, somwhere between the realms of Bologna and Reggio, there was Rubikdom, the realm of the Great Beast (aka: Cioppi).
It wasn't a very big place but it was full of wonders and long lost crunchy treats.
The undisputed ruler and protector of the realm, Rubik, ruled over it with an ironpaw.
Whoever dared disobeying him was bitten, scratched or even waken up by jumping on his balls.
It was a reign of terror and unceirtainty.
